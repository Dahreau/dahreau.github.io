About Me

Hello! I'm Daro Samaky, a passionate Full Stack Developer with expertise in creating responsive web applications and interactive digital experiences.

I specialize in modern web technologies including JavaScript, Go, and SQL. My journey in programming began with creating simple websites and evolved into developing complex full-stack applications that solve real-world problems.

My versatility with programming languages is one of my greatest strengths. I've developed the ability to quickly learn and switch between languages like JavaScript, Go, Lua, and SQL, understanding that each language has its specific use cases. This language adaptability allows me to approach problems from multiple perspectives and choose the most efficient solution for each challenge.

Through collaborative projects at Zone01 Coding School, I've developed strong adaptability skills, learning to navigate diverse team dynamics and rapidly adjust to evolving requirements. These group experiences have strengthened my communication abilities and reinforced my capacity to contribute effectively in any team environment.

My self-directed learning style has cultivated strong autonomy, enabling me to independently research solutions, manage complex projects, and continuously expand my skill set without direct supervision.
